The growth of social networks over the last few years has revolutionized the way people interact and industries conduct business. People produce data at an unprecedented rate by interacting, sharing, and consuming content through social networks. Understanding and analyzing  this new type of data to produce clear emotional result  presents challenges and opportunities for researchers  , understating Collective consciousness , marketing analyses and social studies .
in our project we are building a tool that analyze opinions on a given matter by mining data from social networks and processing  them for emotions .
